What is running for brews?

My hobby is Running for Brews in St. Petersburg, FL. It's a great way to exercise and meet new people with the added benefit of beer. I started attending Running for Brews in 2017 and have learned to love running.

What if you don't like running?

Some people don't like running and they think I'm crazy for running in this heat. It's okay because you don't have to run. You can walk, or just sit at the bar and drink. Why not?

What if you don't like beer?

It's okay if you don't like beer. There are non-alcoholic options available.
